III. Epistemological Model (Zanabûn)
Definition:
Knowledge is valid when:
Produced by cognition (Layer 3)
Verified by systemic consistency (Layer 4)
Filtered through ethical intention (Layer 5)
Key Argument:
Pure positivism limits analysis to Layers 1–3.
Hebûn–Zanabûn includes Layers 4–6 as structural components of reality.

Seeks systemic stabilization.
Rasterast is not ideology;
It is a layered analytical method.
