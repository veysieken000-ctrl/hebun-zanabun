## Abstract Örneği

This paper introduces a six-dimensional ontological-epistemological
framework integrating physical, biological, cognitive, normative,
ethical and meta-judgmental layers into a unified formal structure.
We present an axiom system (A1–A12), theorem catalogue (T1–T10),
and a meta-consistency proof establishing internal non-contradiction.
The framework extends beyond reductionist physicalism by introducing
dual-lock normative binding between ethics and judgment.
