# Operators & Axioms

## Operators

x × y : interaction under constraints  
f ∘ g : composition  
x ⊕ y : constructive merge  
x ⊗ L4 : bind under law  
a ▷ k : interpret claim  

## Axioms

AX1: Valid knowledge requires two-subject agreement.

AX2: Common rational ground is non-empty.

AX3: Law-binding does not alter meaning, only constraints.

AX4: Accountable action produces responsibility trace.

AX5: Moral-layer rejection leads to degeneration.
