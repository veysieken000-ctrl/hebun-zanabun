# Zanistarast: A Multi-Layered Civilizational Equilibrium Framework

## Abstract

This paper introduces Zanistarast as a meta-integrative civilizational framework combining ontological grounding (Hebûn), epistemological responsibility (Zanabûn), natural equilibrium modeling (Mabûn), and ethical calibration (Rasterast). The framework proposes a structured integration of material analysis (4D) and moral judgment (5D) within a unified ontological model (6+Tek). Rather than an ideological alternative, Zanistarast is presented as a structural equilibrium system.

---

## 1. Introduction

Contemporary socio-economic systems often separate material growth from ethical calibration and knowledge production from responsibility. This structural fragmentation has generated technological progress alongside moral and ecological imbalance.

Zanistarast proposes a reintegration model grounded in multi-layered structural coherence.
